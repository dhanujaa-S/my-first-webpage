1.Atomic Habits:

what circumstances led the author to write the book?
James clear wrote Atomic Habits after recovering from
a serious injury,using small habits to rebuild his life,which inspired
him to share the power of tiny changes.

What are the important things that Author Focus on?

  1. Tiny Habits=Big results.
  2. Build systems,Not just goals.
  3. Use the Four Laws:obvious,Attractive,Easy,satisfying.
  4. shap your environment to support good habits.
  5. Focus on identity,not outcomes.

My Suggestion:

This book is really useful who want to change their Habits.
